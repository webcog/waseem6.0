Cash On Delivery (COD)

We are offering COD payment method on orders.
  • Pros for COD method are that checkout is quick and you have peace of mind to pay cash on delivery to reduce risk of fraud.
  • Cons for COD method are that you can’t order more than certain limit of amount & weight. Delivery charges for COD shipments are also higher than standard shipping charges.

It’s advised if you are new shopper and have trust issues use COD method. But for big amount & bulky items purchase use other payment methods.
